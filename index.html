Soccerer's Stone

Sorcerer's Stone: J.K. Rowling's enchanting debut introduces us to the magical world of Harry Potter, where a young boy discovers his extraordinary heritage and embarks on a thrilling quest for the legendary Philosopher's Stone.

The Witches

The Witches (Roald Dahl): Roald Dahl weaves a wickedly entertaining tale of a young boy's encounter with witches who plot to eradicate children, showcasing his whimsical storytelling and dark humor.

The Hobbit: J.R.R. Tolkien's enchanting prelude to The Lord of the Rings follows Bilbo Baggins on a thrilling adventure filled with dwarves, dragons, and the discovery of his inner heroism.
